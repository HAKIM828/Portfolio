Project Details

The Moroccan World Cup Lines Charte Graphique represents a meticulously designed brand identity for a transportation company dedicated to connecting tourists and locals between the six stadiums hosting the World Cup in Morocco. The project captures the essence of Moroccan culture through vibrant visual elements, including the bus designs, employee uniforms, wayfinding signage, and other branding components. The charte graphique establishes a cohesive and professional identity that reflects the company's innovative spirit, offering a seamless travel experience with its smart application and website. This project emphasizes user convenience, with features like stadium-specific navigation, nearby bus identification, and comprehensive station information. By combining aesthetic appeal with functionality, the charte graphique serves as the backbone of the company's public image.
