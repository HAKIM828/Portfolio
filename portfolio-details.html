Project Details

This project involved modeling and animating my HP laptop in Blender, showcasing my ability to recreate real-world objects in a 3D environment. I focused on achieving accurate proportions, realistic texturing, and smooth animations to bring the laptop to life. The final result highlights my growing expertise in 3D modeling and rendering while exploring the nuances of Blender's tools. From designing intricate details like the keyboard to animating subtle opening and closing movements, the project reflects my dedication to creating visually appealing and realistic designs.

3D HP laptop Modeling & animation

I undertook this project as a personal challenge to refine my skills in 3D modeling and animation. The HP laptop, being a familiar object, offered the perfect opportunity to focus on precision and creativity. By modeling something I interact with daily, I could pay closer attention to its features and translate them into a digital format. This project also helped me better understand the workflow of professional product visualization, preparing me for more complex and diverse projects in the future.
