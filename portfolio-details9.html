Project Details

The 3D Glass Cup Modeling project showcases a realistic representation of a transparent glass cup, emphasizing its smooth curves, fine details, and reflective properties. The design demonstrates mastery in handling materials like glass, precise modeling techniques, and realistic rendering to achieve lifelike visuals. The project highlights the elegance and simplicity of everyday objects, turning them into pieces of art through 3D visualization.

3D Glass Cup Modeling

This project was an opportunity to improve my skills in modeling and texturing transparent materials, which are often challenging in 3D design. I wanted to explore the intricacies of light refraction and reflection to create a highly realistic glass cup. It also serves as a practical example of how such assets can be used in product visualization, advertisements, and branding materials.
